About Our School

Quality Education Since 1989

Morning Star Secondary Boarding School is a private, English-medium school that believes in the power of discipline, dedication, and modern learning. From nursery to Class 12, it's not just a school—it's a second home for over 800+ bright students.

Under the visionary leadership of Phadindra Adhikari (Principal) and Indra Dorje Lama (Coordinator), the school is guided by experienced teachers who don't just teach from textbooks, but from the heart.

  • Annapurna Rural Municipality-2, Kaski, Dhikur Pokhari
  • Established in 1989 A.D.
  • English medium instruction
  • Nursery to Grade 12
